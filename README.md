# Superstore Sales Dashboard

**Tools:** Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Formulas, Dashboard)

**Dataset:** 9,000+ sales transactions including Region, State, City, Order ID, Sales, Profit, Category, and Sub-Category.

---

## **Project Overview**
This project demonstrates an end-to-end Excel data analysis workflow:
- Data cleaning and preparation
- Pivot Tables for sales and profit analysis
- Charts for visualization
- Interactive dashboard with KPIs and slicers
- Insights from the analysis

---

## **Key Features**
- **Total Sales & Profit KPIs** – Track overall revenue and profitability  
- **Sales by Region** – Identify top-performing regions  
- **Sales by Category & Sub-Category** – Analyze product performance  
- **Yearly Sales Trends** – Show seasonality and growth patterns  
- **Top 10 Products** – Highlight best-selling products contributing most to revenue  

---

## **Insights**
- West region contributes **35% of total sales**  
- Technology category drives the **highest profit**  
- Top 10 products generate **~40% of total revenue**
